## Trap door
Encrypt: ${\underbrace{\Huge m}_{\text{message}}}^{e} \mod \text{N} = \underbrace{\Huge c}_{\text{cipher}}$

<hr/>

Decript: ${\underbrace{\Huge c}_{\text{cipher}}}^{d} \mod \text{N} = \underbrace{\Huge m}_{\text{message}}$

$
\begin{cases}
	m^{e} \mod \text{N} = c
	\\
	c^{d} \mod \text{N} = m
\end{cases}
\\
m^{e} \mod \text{N} = c \rArr m^{e} = t*\text{N}+c \rArr c = m^{e}-t*\text{N}
\\
c = m^{e}-t*\text{N} \to c^{d} \mod \text{N} = m \rArr {(m^{e}-t\text{N})}^{d} \mod \text{N}= m
\\
{(m^{e}-t\text{N})}^{d}=C_{0}^{d}{m^{e}}^d(-t\text{N})^0+C_{1}^{d}{m^{e}}^{(d-1)}(-t\text{N})^1+...+C_{d}^{d}{m^{e}}^{0}(-t\text{N})^d=m^{ed}
\\
{(m^{e}-t\text{N})}^{d}=m^{ed} \to {(m^{e}-t\text{N})}^{d} \mod \text{N}= m^{ed} \mod \text{N} = m
$

## Euler Theorem
$
m,n \in N^{+} \And (m,n)=1
\\
m ^ {\phi(n)} \equiv 1 (\mod \text{N})=m ^ {\phi(n)} \equiv 1 \mod \text{N}
$

## Euler Theorem + Trap door
$
m ^ {\phi(n)} = 1 \mod \text{N} \rArr m ^ {\phi(n)k} = {(1 \mod \text{N})}^{k}
\\
\rArr m ^ {\phi(n)k} = 1 \mod \text{N} \rArr m ^ {\phi(n)k} * m = (1 \mod \text{N})*m
\\
\rArr m ^ {\phi(n)k+1} = m \mod \text{N} \rArr m ^ {\phi(n)k+1} \mod \text{N} = m
\\
\begin{cases}

\\
m ^ {\phi(n)k+1} \mod \text{N} = m

\rArr ed=\phi(n)k+1
